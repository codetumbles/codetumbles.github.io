Hi, I'm Usman Akram.

I'm a Machine Learning Engineer at **Rakuten**, where I work on the systems behind
foundation language models. Most of my work these days is inference
optimization, distributed training, and the messy parts underneath. Before LLMs,
I worked on ad recommendation, and before that, I led teams building scalable
ML platforms for training and inference.

I've been on both sides of the picture, so some days you'll find me designing
ML systems that handle petabytes with insane throughput and tight latency, and
other days I'm spending a weekend rummaging through a research paper or
reading the code behind stuff like FlashAttention.
